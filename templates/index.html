Flower Classification

Iris Setosa

Iris Setosa is a species of iris native to various regions. It is characterized by its unique color pattern and distinct features. It is widely recognized for its beauty and has been a subject of fascination for many researchers and enthusiasts. The Iris Setosa is known for its vibrant colors and delicate petals, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.

Iris Virginica

Iris Virginica is a species of iris native to North America. It is known for its elegant appearance and vibrant colors. The Iris Virginica is characterized by its tall stems and large, showy flowers. It is often found in wetland habitats and is highly valued for its ecological significance. The Iris Virginica is a symbol of beauty and resilience, representing the natural diversity and richness of the environment.

Iris Versicolor

Iris Versicolor, also known as the Harlequin Blueflag, is a species of iris native to North America. It is characterized by its striking colors and unique petal patterns. The Iris Versicolor is often found in wetland areas and is considered an indicator of environmental health. This flower holds cultural significance in many indigenous communities and is admired for its beauty and adaptability.
